Now in its fourth year, the Hackference Conference portion is being held in its most beautifully unique venue yet! We are taking over The Electric in Birmingham, to have an amazing cast of speakers cover a full spectrum of topics. From languages and paradigms to API's and hobby projects, there is something for everyone. So if you are new to programming and computers, an industry expert or anywhere inbetween the two - you do not want to miss out.

As usual, we'll make sure you will have the best conference lunch you'll ever have, enough snacks and drinks to keep your mind fresh, lots of chances for networking and free entry into our amazing after-party. And you'll get priority tickets to our legendary Hackathon when those tickets are released!

The Venue

This year we are proud to be holding the conference at The Electric, the oldest working cinema in the UK. You'll be treated by its beautiful rooms, comfortable cinema seats, super location - directly behing Grand Central Station (perfect for those who are using public transport) and complimentary snacks, drinks and lunch.
